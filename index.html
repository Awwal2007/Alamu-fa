About The Academy

Alamu Football Academy

Alamu Football Academy is the premier destination for youth football development in Iwo Land. Established with a vision to nurture the next generation of football stars, combines athletic training with character education.

Our academy is known for its progressive training methods, commitment to player development, and efforts to promote sportsmanship and discipline globally. We are dedicated to fostering unity and progress through the beautiful game.

Since inception,has witnessed significant success in youth leagues, talent discovery, and community engagement. The academy is also recognized for its advocacy for peace and initiatives to empower youth through sports.
